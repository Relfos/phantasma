Phantasma

Next-generation email, using the power of the NEO blockchain.
Decentralized, free, fast and secure.

What is Phantasma

Relying in third party services for something as critical and personal like email is dangerous.
Companies can be hacked, bankruptcies can happen and goverment entities can force access into emails.

Phantasma brings an complete email system running in the blockchain. And the best part, it still works exactly like you expect email to work.

You are the owner

Phantasma runs on top of a crypto-wallet address, using the NEO blockchain.
You own the wallet private key, thus you own the mailbox content.

Everything is encrypted, and without the private key nobody can read it or steal it from you, not even the Phantasma developers.

Not only for email

Email in the current state is built on top of an legacy system with decades of existence. Phantasma breaks free from that system, and introduces a new protocol for content.

Here come's a new way to distribute messages, photos, invoices, movies and more!

Gateway to the outside

Unlike competitors, Phantasma mailboxes are standard email addresses, thus can communicate with email systems outside the blockchain.

Besides that, Phantasma can do more than email, so other outside systems can be potentially be connected to a Phantasma mailbox.

A new way to pay

Phantasma makes leverage of the NEO blockchain features, which makes possible to transparently transfer assets between users, since every Phantasma mailbox is also a NEO wallet.

With the introduction of the invoice messages, users will be able to request money from other Phantasma users easily.

Powered by IPFS

Phantasma is using IPFS, a distributed file-system that we use as storage backend. The use of an out-of-chain distributed file system lets us keep the NEO blockchain free from bloat, and at the same time lets us keep the GAS cost low when interacting with smart contracts, letting us have a free and fast communication platform.

And of course, while distributed, all file content used with the Phantasma is encrypted to ensure that no one has access to it except yourself.

Run your own nodes

Decentralization is the modus operandi of Phantasma, thus any organization or individual is encouraged to run his own nodes.

Thanks to the token ecosystem created by Phantasma, node-operators who run their own Phantasma services will be reap rewards in form of PHI tokens.

Platform Roadmap

The development of the Phantasma platform will be divided into multiple phases.
This roadmap is intendend as an outline for how Phantasma intends to continue extending and expanding the platform to achieve the overall goal of decentralized communication.

  • March 2017

  • Initial R&D

    Research and development of the Phantasma protocol and Blockchain technology.

  • September 2017

  • City Of Zion Submission

    Phantasma submitted to City of Zion dApps contest.

  • October 2017

  • Public Announcement

    Crowdsale announcement, start of marketing phase.

  • Whitepaper Release

    Release of the Phantasma whitepaper to the public.

  • November 2017

  • City of Zion Results

    Winners of the City of Zion competition will be announced.

  • Phantasma App - Beta release

    A beta version of the Phantasma mobile app will be released, showcasing the core features.

  • Crowdsale Start

    Crowdsale will open via NEO smart contract. Only individuals who signed up to the whitelist can participate.

  • Crowdsale End

    PHI tokens will be distributed to participants of the crowdsale, and refunds from sales over the cap will be processed.

  • Q1 2018

  • Exchange Trading

    The PHI token will be available for trade in exchanges.

  • Phantasma Gateway

    Official release of Phantasma Gateway, to allow any individual or company to run their own Phantasma node.

  • Protocol Update - Files

    Adds a new message type to the protocol, allowing sending of files.

  • Phantasma App - Official Launch

    Release of the official Phantasma mobile app for iOS and Android, improved with feedback from the community.

  • Q2 2018

  • Protocol Update - Invoices

    Adds a new message type to the protocol, allowing sending invoices, donations and payments.

  • Third-Party Integration

    Start of work to integrate third-party services, eg: plugins for existing email clients.

The roadmap above is subject to change, according with user demand and market conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I participate in the crowdsale?

To participate in the token sale, you first must register in the whitelist, then when the crowdsale start you must send us NEO tokens using a compatible NEO wallet. Our wallet address will be sent via email before the token sale. For more information on the contribution process, check out our guide.

To obtain NEO tokens, buy from an exchange, such as Binance or Bittrex. Once NEO tokens have been obtained from the exchange, transfer NEO to your personal wallet.

Can I send NEO tokens from an exchange for the token sale?

NO – any tokens sent from an exchange to the token sale wallet will most likely be lost.

NEO tokens must be sent using NEO wallet compatible with crowdsales. Check out our guide for more information.

What exactly is Phantasma?

Phantasma is a new protocol for sending messages from one source to one or multiple destinations. With Phantasma it is possible to implement emails and other message-based systems on top of a blockchain.
